IELTS writing part 1

Posted on 2018-07-17

[TOC]

KEY

  • The task is to describe what you see. Write a report. Don’t give opinions.
  • No conclusion. Instead write a summary(the overview).

Line graph

Bar chart

Pie chart

Table

numbers - describe - compare - changes/trends

Diagram-comparing

Diagram-process

4 paragraphs

  1. Introduction: One sentence, paraphrase the question

  2. Overview(or at end):2 sentences, the main, general things

  3. Details - comparison

  4. Details - trends

Line Chart

  • Compare the lines, but not to describe them separately
  • First, make a very general comparion(Summary)
  • Second, compare the lines at specific points
    • Write at least one thing about each line

Intro-paraphrase the question

  • the graph below shows/ the line graph compares electricity production/ the amount of electricity produced in France using four different sources of power between 1980 and 2012/ over a period of 32 years

Overview(highest/lowest)

It is clear that nuclear power was by far the most important means of electricity generation over the period shown. Renewables provided the lowest amount of electricity in each year

Detail - compare the lines

  • In 1980, thermal power stations were the main source of electricity in France, generating around 120 terawatt hourse of power.
  • Nuclear and xxx power station produced just under 75 xxx hours of electricity each, and renewables provided a negligible amount.
  • Just one year later, nuclear power overtook xxx power as the primary source of electricity
  1. Nuclear power was used to produce 75 terawatt hours of electricity
  2. Nuclear power station produced 75 xxx

Detail -2

  • Between 1980 and 2005, electricity production from nuclear rose dramatically to a peak of 430 terawatt hours.
  • By contrast, the figure for thermal power fell to only 50 terawatt hours in 1985, and remain at this level for the rest of the period.
  • xxx power generation remained relatively stable, at between 50 and 80 terawatt hours, for the whole 32-year period, but renewable electricity production saw only a small rise to approximately 25 terawatt hours by 2012

Bar chart

Introduction

  • global sales of/the number of mobile phones sold worldwide
  • the top five mobile phone brands/the five most popular manufacturers
  • between 2009 and 2013/ in the years 2009, 2011 and 2013

Overview

  • xxx saw the biggest rises in sales over the 5-year period

Detail -1

  • almost double the number of handsets sold by the second most successful manufacturer.
  • By 2013, Samsung had become the market leader/dominate the market/ be predominant with sales reaching 450 million units.

Detail -2

  • The other three top selling mobile phone/handsets brands
  • sold around xx, xxx, xx respectively
  • overtook the other two vendors while LG saw declining sales and the figures for ZTE rose only slightly.

Vocabulary

  • brands, manufacturers, companies, vendors
  • close to, almost, approximately, around

Pie chart

  • pie chart don’t always show changes over time
  • compare percentages(and numbers)
  • household spending pattern/five categories of household expenditure
  • Don’t describe each country or each year separately. Compare year and coutries together
  • Mention 5 categories
  • Divide the categories into 2 groups for the two ‘details’ paragraphs
  • The category called ‘other’ is not important
Overview
  • It is noticeable that the proportion of spending on food fell in both countries
  • over the 28-year period/spent a significantly larger percentage of their household budgets
Detail
  • talk about numbers
voc
  • spending, expenditure, spent, costs
  • proportion of spending
  • the equivalent figure for a UK home
  • fell, rose, saw an increase in
  • spent half as much in relative terms on recreation/leisure time
  • leisure accounted for the highest proportion

Table

  • the same information as a line graph, bar chart or pie chart
  • changes over time(increase decrease)/comparisons
  • select key numbers

Voc

  • the top five countries = the five highest ranking countries in terms of
  • in 2012 and 2013 = over a period of two years
  • It is clear that xxx in the years 2012 and 2013. However, xxxx over the same period
  • earned by far the most
  • revenue from tourism
  • 2013 saw a rise of between 1 and 4 million tourist visits to each country, with the exception of China, which received 2 million fewer visitors than in the previous year
  • saw a rise, increase, rising from, rose by

Keys

  • can’t mention every number. chose around 10 out of the total 20 numbers

2 different type chart

  • look for one main feature in each chart(summary)
  • describe specific numbers for each chart separately

voc

  • men and women/females and males
  • attend various evening courses/took four different evening classes(specific numbers)
  • the ages of these course participants/the age profile of these attendees
  • Younger adults were in the minority, with only 11% of students aged 20 to 29, and only 5% aged under 20.
  • the majority of/were in the minority
  • to be precise
  • significantly more, higher, highest
  • over 40 years of age/50 or more/between 40 and 49/aged 20 to 29/age under 20

Process diagram

difference

  • no numbers
  • can’t compare anything
  • can’t describe trend(increase, decrease)

talking about

  • how many steps
  • where the process begins and ends
  • each step in the process

‘step’ language

  • At the first stage in the process
  • The process begins with
  • Secondly, Finally
  • The second step involves
  • Next, then, after that
  • At the following stage

Some words

  • steps/stages in the process of
  • consists of four distinct stages
  • it requires five steps
  • raw materials, end products
  • production, manufacturing
  • turn used glass into new products
  • at the first stage, next, then, begin with
  • are mixed, is heated, can be shaped
  • is sorted according to
  • can be moulded to create
  • are mixed -> this mxiture
  • collection -> the collected glass
  • is melted -> the resulting molten glass
