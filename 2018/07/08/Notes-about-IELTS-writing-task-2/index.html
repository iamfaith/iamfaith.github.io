Notes about IELTS writing task 2

Posted on 2018-07-08

Minimum 250 words for 40 minutes

criteria：1 Task response 2 Coherence and cohesion 3 vocabulary 4 grammer（less important）

Timing（40 minutes）

Planning - 10 minutes

Introduction - 2sentences - 5 minutes - to be short

Four types of questions:

1.Discussion (Discuss both views and give your own opinion)

Topic - study in groups or alone

Answer - sometimes better alone, usually better in a group

People have different views about the effectiveness of group study as opposed to working alone. While there are some benefits to studying independently, I believe that group work is usually more productive.

2.Opinion (To what extent do you agree or disagree)

Topic - community services for all teenagers

Answer - 3 choices: agree, disagree, or balanced opinion(when possible)

It is sometimes argued that high school students should be made to do some work in their local communities. (agree) I completely argee that this kinds of scheme would be a good idea. (disagree) In my opinion, it would be wrong to force teenagers to do any kind of unsalaried work. (balanced) While I disagree with the idea of making such programmes compulsory , I do believe that voluntary community service could benefit young people.

3.Problem and solution (what measures can be taken to tackle this problem)

Topic - criminals reoffend

Answer - several reasons, a variety of measures(governments, communities)

It is ture that punishments do not always deter criminals from committing more crimes. There are various reasons why offenders repeatedlly break the law, but governments could certainly take steps to address this issue.

4.Tow-part question (what factors contribute to job satisfaction? How realistic is the expectation of job saftisfaction for all workers?)

Plan: Topic - job satisfaction

Answer - several factors, unrealistic / impossible

Work plays a central role in our lives, and we could all like to feel fulfilled professionally. While a variety of factors may lead to job satisfaction, it would be unrealistic to expect everyone to be happy at work.

Two main paragraphs - 5 sentences each - 20minutes - 90 to 100 words each

Firstly, Secondly, Finally (Linking word don’t help your VOC score)(advantages/disadvantages/problem/solutions)

3-min plan: -disagree

  • school timetable is full, no time for community service

    There are several reasons why I would argue aganis having compulsory community services for secondary school students.Firstly, the school curriculum is already full with important academic subjects, such as maths, sciences and languages. For example, I remember having an extremely busy timetable when I was at high school, and it would not have been possible to add to it.(PS: Firstly - explain more, or add example)

  • students’ work in other subjects would be affected.

    Secondly, students’ performance in other subjects would be affected if valuable study time were taken by charity work or neighborhood improvement schemes.

  • teenagers might not want to do it(reluctant, no motivation)

    Finally, I believe that teenage students would be reluctant to take part in any program of obligatory work, and this could lead to poor motivation and even bad behavior.

Idea, Explain, Example (one idea/a reason/an opinion)

  • voluntary (not compulsory) community service is positive

    On the other hand, the opportunity to do voluntary community service counld be extremely positive for high school students.

  • students more motivated if they can choose

    By making these programmes optional, school would ensure that only motivated students took part.

  • gain work experience, selft confidence, skills

    These young people would gain valuable experience in an adult working environment, which could help to build their self confidence and enhance their skills.

  • good for CVs, career, unversity admissions, employers

    Having such experience and skills on their CVs could greatly improve school leavers’ career prospects. For example, a period of voluntary work experience might impress a university admissions officer or a future employer.

Conclusion - 1 sentence - 5 minutes

  • never write anything new
  • one sentence: repeat, summarise
  • paraphrasing to show variety

1.Discussion(+ opinion)

there are more benefits than drawbacks

In conclusion, I would argue/I believe that the advantages of using video security systems in public places do outweigh the disadvantages.

2.Opinion

I completely disagree / should not need to contribute to state school through taxes

In conclusion, I do not believe that any financial concessions should be made for people who choose/attend private school.

3.problem and solution

societies can take steps to mitigate / there will undoubtedly be some negative consequences / expect to live longer than ever before

In conclusion, various measures can be taken to tackle the problems that are certain to arise as the poplulations of countries grow older.

4.Tow-part quesiton

a vital part of all human cultures

In conclusion, music is necessary part of human existence, and I believe/would argue that tranditional music should be given more importance/ is more important than modern music.

Example

more effective? study alone : study in groups

  1. Intro: sometimes better alone, usually better in groups
  2. Benefits of alone
    • concentrate better, no distraction, focus on the task, read books or articles, study at own places
    • e.g when revising for an exam, memorize information
  3. Benefits of group study(my view)
    • more ideas, share knowledge, gather more information e.g. research project, learn from each other, more motivating, responsibility to the group, sense of competition
  4. Conclusion: both have benefits, but I prefer group

Opinion essay(do you agree or disagree, not other people)

  1. Intro: government spending agree - public services instead of space projects

    I completely agree with the idea that these are a waste of money, and that the funds should be allocated/used to public services.

  2. explain why ‘space’ spending should be stop/abandoned

    • [x] waste of money when projects fail, expensive(scientists, facilities, equipment), no benefit to normal people, politicians showing power, risk of accidents, deaths e.g. Challenger space shuttle
    • [x] They are simply vanity projects for politicians/ just politician show off

  3. explain why public service spending is better

    • [x] cheaper e.g. doctors, teachers police instead of astronauts, public services impact on everyone, reduce poverty, better quality of life, we all use schools, hospitals, police, roads, etc.
    • [x] If governments reallocated the money spent on space travel and research, many thousands of people could be lifted out of poverty poverty or given a better quality of life.

  4. Conclusion: spend on services that benefit us all

    • benefit all members of society
    • wrong to waste resources

  5. VOC

    • space programs, exploration, missions, projects, travel, research
    • funds should be allocated/ should be abandoned
    • facilities and equipment come at a huge cost
    • vanity projects for politician
