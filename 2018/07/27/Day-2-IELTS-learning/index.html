Day 2 Speaking

Posted on 2018-07-27

sleep

本题为日常类话题，答案贴合日常、讲出真实情况即可，不要一味求新求变。时态以现在时为主。

How many hours do you sleep every day? Why?

I need to sleep at least 8 hours every day including a cozy catnap after lunch, or otherwise I would feel drowsy(昏昏欲睡的) all day long. insomnia失眠症

Is it necessary to have enough sleep?

If you wanna keep fit, this is my primal suggestion. Enough sleep means a complete rest for all body functions so that you can easily enjoy a faster metabolism（代谢） and own a better physique.

Is taking a nap important?

At least for me, yes. A short but sound nap after lunch every day esp after taking in a bit much starch which increases your blood sugar is essential. I can be reassured（安心） that I will have a productive afternoon after taking one.

Do old people sleep a lot?

As far as I know, no. Old people sleep much less than their younger generations or themselves in younger years. like my nanny who sleeps only 3 hours every day with the help of some hypnotics（安眠药）.

Do you think staying up late is a good thing?

It never is. Staying up destroys your biological clock and your body’s circadian rhythm 昼夜节律 would be in a mess. Having said that, I know how it feels to live in this modern society with excessive work to do, even at night.

good parent

Describe someonewho you think is a good parent.
You should say:
Who the person is
What kind of person he/she is
How you know this person
Why you think he/she is good
And explain how youfeel about him/her (why you think they are a good parent).

  1. 本题的A是someone，描述人；B是whoyou think is a good parent，限制此人的身份和属性。可以理解为人物+事件结合类题目，也可理解为单纯人物类题目。
  2. 提纲建议：
    a) 描述这位parent的基本信息，外貌、性格，引出好家长的最大特征
    b) 详述好家长原因或小故事
    c) 对ta的评价，适当升华
  3. 时态建议：描述人物特征时用一般现在时，讲故事用过去时，最后的评价如果涉及到类别问题，可用一般现在时；述及对现在的影响可用现在完成时。
    其他：好家长可以是自己家长，也可以是地球上任何一个孩子爸妈，所以不必拘泥于人物选择。比如串题时用一个名人（受欢迎的人）的材料，ta同时也上过“爸爸去哪儿”之类的节目显示出好家长的一面，也OK。

I can’t think of a better epitome缩影 of parenting than my mom. She is THE perfect parent. My mom is 53 years old, but she looks so young that when we stroll two abreast, we are often misrecognized as siblings. Without doubt, I consider her the best parent in the world because she’s always respected me and loved me for who I am in my childhood and during my formative years.

You know how kids are often picky eater right? Broccoli, green peppers and tomatoes used to be utterly repulsive厌恶 for me, and unlike other moms who authoritatively forced their kids to eat them, my mom didn’t think of it as a serious matter and told me that I needed to eat other vegetables sufficiently instead, because her theory is fibers and vitamins are something we’re looking for in vegetables, and there are tons of vegetables full of these nutrients as long as parents don’t make the mistake of substituting unhealthy foods. She thinks that it isn’t a big deal that children develop food preferences and go through them in stages. So in my home, we never turn mealtimes into unpleasant occasions. And since I could never find any junk food at home, fried chicken, chips and candy bars were never in my diet. Interestingly, the vegetables I hated back then are my favorites now.

Aside from that, my mom always fostered my independence. She thinks that children push for independence because it is part of human nature to want to feel in control rather than to feel controlled by someone else. And that’s why she never entered my room without knocking first, never read my diaries, supported my part-time jobs and my choice of girlfriends.

I’ve become this confident, altruistic independent man because of her. I am so grateful that I could be able have her in my life. I love my mom.

I’ve had better

表达“不喜欢、不乐意、不稀罕甚至糟糕透顶了”的委婉讲法，“嗯，我见过听过试过用过吃过更好的呢”，一点点傲娇，a little bit sassy，这就是这个表达法所显露出的心里状态。而这里的动词had也可以换成其他动词的过去分词，如I’ve seen better. I’ve heard better, etc. 另外，还可以在better后面继续加名词，如I’ve had better days.
To be honest, I’ve heard better ones. The three characters in my name are all mediocre.

  • How’s the new job treating you?
    Well, I’ve had better.
    The whole point of Silicon Valley is that people jump between jobs to kind of mix up the pot.

from scratch

从零开始，白手起家。这种从无到有的过程，就叫from scratch。它的愿意是指做菜的时候所有材料都自备，而不是使用店里卖的半成品。后来则所有从零开始做的事情都可以用这个表达形容。

Jack Ma, my favorite celebrity in China, a former English tutor, built his company from scratch.

After knowing that we needed to work on this project, we did it from scratch, searching for materials, preparing for the presentation, etc.
