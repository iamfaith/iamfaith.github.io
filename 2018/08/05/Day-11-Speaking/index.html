Day 11 Speaking

Posted on 2018-08-05

【P1】通讯类：信

Do you write many letters?

You probably won’t believe me, but I’ve never written any letters in my life and I don’t think I ever will. I send a lot of emails but not traditional hand-written letters, no.

Do you prefer to write letters by hand or use a computer?

Definitely use a computer. If you own a computer, I will suggest you do the same. Writing letters by hand is unproductive and relatively more expensive. Not to mention no one could ever decipher my scrawl.

What do you usually write about?

I write about my latest life to my mom, my academic problems to my advisor, some strong language to spam, all by email.

Is it hard to think of what to write?

As a perfectionist, I would say, yes. A grammatical lapse, a bad word choice, and most of all, spelling mistakes, ewww, all drive me crazy. And that’s why I always revisewhatever I write.

How do you feel when you receive a letter?

Subjunctive mood, please. Since I haven’t received a letter for a million years, I might feel scared, to be honest. What if there’s a letter bomb in it?

Do you think people will still write letters in the future?

Well, we may stick to hand-written letters on negligible occasions, like, I mean, who would wanna receive a love email, right? When you wanna show your sincerity, your affections and your emotions, you’d better use your decrepit pen once again.

【P2】事件类：与老亲人做事

Describe something you enjoy doing with an old person in your family.

  • What it is
  • How often you do it
  • Who you do it with
  • Why you enjoy doing it with this person
  1. 本题的A是something，描述事物；B是you enjoy doing with an old person in your family, 限制内容。
  2. 提纲建议：
    • 介绍这件事及其特征，引出与老亲人做
    • 详述做这件事通常的时间、地点、频次，也可以讲一次具体经历
    • 讲述enjoy的表现和原因，收尾
  3. 时态建议：这道题一定是一般现在时为主，因为you enjoy doing，要讲成一个routine。但不代表不能讲过去的某一次，还是可以的，讲的时候用过去时。
  4. 其他：本题千万不能一来就讲与这位老亲人某次做这件事的经历，讲得再精彩也没用。一定要从routine讲起，说成一件经常（或规律性）在一起做的事。然后为了串题方便，还是可以讲某一次。

I really enjoy teaching my grandma how to use her iPhone I bought her for her 70’s birthday. We’ve been doing it on and off for a year now, and she’s already been familiar with sending voice messages via WeChat, the most popular instant messaging app in China with 10 billion users around the world. And next up, I’m gonna teach her to use mapping apps to find her way back home from the park she goes to to practice her Erhu. And my goal is to transform her from a mobile newbie to a smartphone expert!

It’s sort of a routine now, since my parents and I go to her place on last Sunday of each month and have a fancy meal together. After dinner, my mom is in charge of cleaning the pots and pans which is supposed to be my job but I shirk it and resort to helping granny with her new phone. It works all the time. Seeing my grandma and me having fun bonding makes my mom shrug and straight to the kitchen. So, back to Grandma. I always ease her in by doing something she does with her old flip phone, like making a phone call, add a contact and things. Of course, she’s too old to read even the biggest fonts. That’s why I gave up text messages and turned to voice ones.

It can be frustrating sometimes like the time I taught her to send voice messages. I explained how to send one by clicking the green icon, finding my contact, holding the small virtual button on bottom, speaking and releasing it. Baby steps, I thought, would definitely do the trick. But she would always miss a step or two. I was ready to give up when I suddenly received her first 5s message in which she reminded me to put on more clothes cause it’s getting colder.

Turned out she wanted to learn it in order to take better care of me and our family. I felt guilty and so determined that I would spend more time with her with more patience.

【P2】事件类：优质服务

Describe an occasion where you received a good service (from a company or shop)

  • What the service was
  • When and where it happened
  • Whom you were together with
  • How you felt about it
  • And explain why you think it was good service
  1. 本题的A是an occasion，描述一个场合；B是where you received a good service, 限制内容。
  2. 提纲建议：
    • 记叙文，开门见山讲事情的时间、地点、人物
    • 详述接受好服务的经过
    • 简述内心感受，可升华
  3. 时态建议：这道题一定是一般过去时，但同时也要注意时态的灵活性。如果说到与去其他店（公司）的对比，那么应该用过去完成时。讲对好服务的一般性评价时，用一般现在时和现在完成时。
  4. 其他：本体是比较简单的记叙文，直接以记叙文的时间地点人物起因经过结果的公式展开叙述即可。当然，好的故事是一定有转折有亮点的。

Well, to be honest, almost everybody has experienced this acute frustration that comes with lousy service. We navigate endless phone menus while in hope of talking to a human. The cable guy who’s supposed to show up between noon and 4 p.m. is late. We sit at a restaurant with a bowl of soup in front of us, but there’s no spoon in sight. And sometimes you will be like, is good service really this rare? Well, it is. But I did experience superb service just last week.

So my little gang and me went to this Italian restaurant near to the campus. It is small but cozy, not at all swanky, and completely busy. We were totally amazed by how wonderful the dishes were, the Panzanella salad, Bruschetta, Focaccia and Carbonara were alluring and palatable. The chef sent out a miniature cornucopia of desserts to our table on the house. It wasn’t big, but did include miscellaneous fruits and nuts.

But what impressed us as much as the generous gift was that, at the start of the meal, a vegetarian roommate asked about meat-free offerings. When those sweets arrived at meal’s end, the server subtly pointed out which dessert was made with gelatin to the meat-eschewing diner. We all felt taken care of, our needs anticipated before we had to voice them.

In all, we were all warm and snug in the chilled atmosphere, eating nice food and served by smiley waiters. No wonder we’ve become regulars.

【P2】事件类：未来旅行计划

Describe a trip that you plan to go on in the near future.

  • Where this place is
  • Who you would like to go with
  • What would you do there
  • And explain why you would like to go there
  1. 本题的A是a trip，描述旅程；B是that you plan to go on in the near future, 限制时间。
  2. 提纲建议：
    • 介绍旅程的目的地、时间、跟谁去等基本信息
    • 详述旅程计划的过程、原因以及要做的事，突出特征
    • 简述感受，发表感叹，适度升华
  3. 时态建议：本题时态需要特别注意。Plan to go on in the near future, 既可以用一般现在时讲述“计划”的内容，也可以用将来时或虚拟语气描述这场旅行。
  4. 其他：本题的重点是描述这一trip，一定要注意时态，不是过去时。尤其想串题的话，不要和已经发生过的trips搞混了，当然旅程基本信息仍然能串，但必须改动时态和叙述方式。另外，凡计划的制定，一定有其根据，讲的时候一定要把想法的来源说明白。注意，trip不一定是旅游。

So, I plan to go on a trip to Shandong province, one coastal province in the East of China. As so you know, I was invited to a national summit for videobloggers to Jinan, the capital of Shandong province. It will be a 5-day trip next September, and I’m thinking the first 2 days for the meeting, and the last three, well, I’ll have some fun. A visit to the renowned Mount Tai seems nice.

I would like to go with 2 other excellent vloggers in Beijing, each owning a fantastic account on Weibo, the Chinese equivalent of Twitter. For the summit, I need to prepare for a 10-minute presentation, introducing the development of my account, playing some of my sample videos and sharing my experience on video-shooting and editing. Which will be, well, dull enough, right? I will be sitting in the conference room, or in the audience seats whatever, twiddling with my pen, thinking about what I will go see for the other half of that trip.

Trust me, I’ve already done the elaboration of my itinerary for the other 3 days. Such a shame that I’ve never been to the most prestigious mountain in here, which bears both magnificent natural and cultural significance. What I’m looking forward to the most is the legendary sunrise on the top. I heard from friends that come from Shandong that The best spot for watching the sunrise is Sun-viewing Peak, southeast of Jade Emperor Peak. Waiting for sunrise can be a little boring, but the wait is worth it when you see the glorious sun peaking over the mountains. Just the thought of it makes me zealous and chilled.

In all, I’m so gonna wind down during the trip and I can’t wait.

【每日表达】句型、习语、小幽默

full steam ahead

at full tilt

  • 这是英语中表示速度快的非常有意思的两个相近的表达法，都表示全力以赴，冲刺。为什么说有趣呢，因为它们在表示速度的同时，还表示竭尽全力，用尽力气的意思。在咱们学习英语的过程中，有时候真的需要给自己打打鸡血，看看自己的能力究竟有没有上限。Full steam ahead既可以用作定语，也可以用作状语哦。
  • We’ll have to go full steam ahead if we want to get this done by the deadline!
  • Now that obstacle is out of the way, it’s full steam ahead to get the job finished.
  • Public transportation in my hometown has been developing at full tilt.

anything but

all but

  • 这是一对比较考验逻辑思维的习语，但表示的意思都非常简单。记住公式：anything but = not at all. all but = almost. 仔细分析一下它们的内在逻辑哦。
  • Without you the job would’ve been all but impossible.
  • Though he said he was happy, he looked anything but.
  • anything but… 是“除…之外任何事（物）都…， 根本不…”－－就是我一点都不快乐 nothing but…只有， 只不过； 无非－－－就是我很快乐

【P3技巧】常识类与列举类

基本信息类 Giving basic information

  • 按P1思路回答

常识类 Evoking common sense

  • 按实际情况回答，不逞强，注意现在时态
  • Do Chinese people eat special food in traditional festivals?
  • Is American fast food popular in China?
  • When do Chinese kids start to learn English?

列举类 Enumerating items

  • 按照题目要求列举3个以上（可分类别，也可不分类别），然后详述其中1到2项
  • What wedding gifts are popular in China?
  • What kinds of clothes do people like to wear on formal occasions?
  • What kinds of books can boost people’s imagination?
  • What types of people become famous?
  • What kinds of special food do Chinese eat in traditional festivals?

【纠音跟读】连读与变音2

/t/ + /j/ = /ʧ/

  • Don’t you like it?
  • You have come back, haven’t you?
  • Is that your final answer?
  • Actually
  • Natural
  • Congratulations!

/d/ + /j/ = /ʤ/

  • Did you see that?
  • Where did you go?
  • We followed your instructions.
  • Education
  • Individual
  • Gradually

/s/ + /j/ = /ʃ/

  • Am I the only one who’s late here? Yes, youare.
  • You need to pass your test this year.
  • I’m not gonna guess your age.
  • I miss you.
  • Bless you.
  • Insurance

/z/ + /j/ = /ʒ/

  • How’s your family?
  • How was your trip?
  • When’s your birthday?
  • He says you’re ok.
  • Who does your hair?
  • Casual
  • Usually
  • Version
