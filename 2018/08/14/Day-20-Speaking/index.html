【P1】时间类：准时

时间类题目包括准时、时间管理、效率等，这次考的是准时。注意将准时与道德或礼貌联系起来，其他题目都比较日常。

What will you do if you’re waiting for someone?

I will be like, what the heck is he or she doing, and then call or text them to check on them. If they’re ok, I’ll just find things to do like, well, memorize my vocabulary list, since I’ve been preparing for this test.

Do you think it’s important to be punctual?

Absolutely. Punctuality is a virtue. Think about it, if you’re late for others, you’re wasting their time as well as yours. Mutual respect is way more important than many people think.

Do you think people these days are as punctual as they were in the past?

Hm, I’m not sure. I think it’s pretty much the same. A certain portion of people will always be late for stuff, whatever era they’re born in.

How do you remind yourself to be on time?

For most things, I just keep it in mind and remind myself every now and then. As for important events, I always set a reminder on my phone and the ring reminds me of everything.

Why are some people always late?

They’re just lacking in a proper sense of decorum and don’t know about how we should respect each other when interacting with people.

【P1】学业类：高中（中学）

各个阶段的学校都在雅思口语的考察范畴中，本季考的是高中（中学）。记住，high school指的不仅仅是学校，更是指那个时期。high school对应的是美式的高中和英式的中学。

Do you like your high school?

I love my high school, man. My teachers and classmates were nice. Campus was beautiful. Everything was fine. Wait, did I mention I met my wife in high school? It was a cliched love story that you don’t wanna know, but it’s beautiful.

Which subject do you like or dislike most?

English was my favorite and my least favorite subject back then. Let me explain. I was doing bad at it at first and I hated it but then entered this gorgeous and passionate teacher, Miss Lin who saved my last two years of high school. I got some 140 for English in my Gaokao.

Do you still contact with your friends in high school?

I do, actually. I knew my best buds in high school and we still hang out regularly, playing basketball and video games and the like.

What is your high school like?

My high school is pretty typical in China with study being the critical part and occasional partying and stuff. Best three years of my life so far.

I can’t remember clearly I’m afraid. I think we survived the pep talk for military training and got to know each other during lunch. Gosh, it was like millions of years ago.

【P2】媒体类：喜爱的杂志

Describe a magazine that you like (reading)

  • What the magazine is
  • When you started reading this magazine
  • What the content is
  • And explain why you like this magazine
  1. 本题的A是a magazine，描述一本杂志；B是that you like，限制情感倾向。
  2. 提纲建议：
    • 简述这本杂志的名称及其他基本信息
    • 详述杂志的内容、喜欢的原因
    • 总结和感受
  3. 时态建议：介绍杂志的名称、栏目等用现在时，介绍历史用过去时，讲述喜欢的原因用回现在时。
  4. 其他：这是最简单的媒体类问题，只限制了情感倾向，基本上等于没任何限制。这也为我们串题提供了方便。杂志可以是关于任何内容的，如果把对其他东西的介绍稍改一下，就可以当作某本杂志某篇文章的内容。

Well, the magazine I’m gonna introduce here, is the one and only The Economist, an English weekly which to me is the best magazine of its kind. Wait, do you know that the Economist group doesn’t call the weekly magazine, but a newspaper? I heard from myriads of videos and interviews that it started off as a newspaper, and then the habit stuck. But in fact, it’s known as an illustrious magazine.

I started to read this magazine when its circulation was still relatively huge here in China, which hasn’t been the case for some obvious reasons. It was like 4 years ago when I went to a newsstand on a whim and was soon drawn by the cover of one issue – a huge caricature of nuclear bombs. I then flicked through it and soon fell in love with basically all columns featuring Leaders, News and analyses of different regions, International, Finance and Economics, Science and Technology, Obituary and my favorite, Special Report. I was standing there reading it for 2 hours, impressed by its wit and insight. I had to buy it, right?

The best part of it is how although it has many individual columns, by tradition and current practice the magazine ensures a uniform voice—aided by the anonymity of writers—throughout its pages as if most articles were written by a single author or something, which may be perceived to display dry, understated wit, and precise use of language.

Well, as a layman, I find it hard to understand some articles concerning economics, for its treatment of economics presumes a working familiarity with fundamental concepts of classical economics. Like it never explains terms like invisible hand, macroeconomics, or demand curve, and may take just six or seven words to explain the theory of comparative advantage.

Besides, its editors surely have a wry sense of humor. Many articles include some witticism; image captions are often humorous puns and the letters section usually concludes with an odd or light-hearted letter. Some take them as an offence, but I like’em.

Reading is a great habit and not everyone has it. I am lucky in that regards that I have formed the habit of reading different types of books and magazines from an early age. Now, this is a pleasant moment for me to describe my habit of reading a magazine and my favourite magazine. Besides reading books, I also love reading magazines. They contain a good number of interesting elements to entertain the readers.
I regularly read the Reader’s Digest of Australia and this is the best magazine in my opinion. This is a standard magazine to go through and pass the leisure hours. Besides, this is an enriched magazine that deals with some specific issues and themes. As a result, the readers of the magazine come to know about a lot of things from a single place. It tells the readers about health, shopping, travelling, living at homes and more other issues.
Actually, there is no exact information that which types of people read the magazine. But it is anticipated that the Australians who have crossed their 30s are highly interested in this Reader’s Digest. In fact, they are the people who need some real-life information to deal with ‘situations’ in their life. So, as part of their external needs, they take help from the magazine. The Home Living section of the magazine is particularly useful for the women. They can get ideas to improve their living conditions at home. Besides, they also can generate newer ideas and implementation them on decorating their respective households through the earned knowledge from the magazine. This is also a great resource for the adventurers. The travelling section deals with the travels and adventures and they also provide numerous valuable tips and tricks to be applied in trips.
The magazine is extremely popular in Australia and it has gained popularity by its own capacity. The magazine comes with a nice cover and size, Besides, this is also available in online. So, if someone misses the printed version of the magazine, this is easier to read the articles and stories on its online version. Further, the magazine is popular for some other reasons. This is a special type of magazine that deals with most necessary issues for each of the Australians. It deals with recipes, health, travel, home living and other important aspects of human life. Since we all are to remain busy with our professional engagement, it really becomes hard to take decisions on any important issues, and this is the only magazine that comes with necessary articles, tips and other ways to help in dealing with the situations. Hence, it is a popular magazine.
I love to read books, particularly novels. But this is the only magazine that has attracted my attention because it comes with the necessary things in need. I also love to travel but managing days for a long travel is really tougher for me. With the ideas of travelling tips, I plan for some small trips nearby. Besides, my wife also loves to read the recipe section. She, in fact, loves to learn cooking using the recipes provide on the magazine. Both of us read the magazine and have subscribed to it. Now, it appears that if I had not read the magazine, my life could have been incomplete in some sense. I am really fond of this magazine.

The magazine I often read is “Medical Technology”. People who are medical laboratory technicians love to read this magazine. It’s a monthly magazine. It is quite popular in Hong Kong. Almost every registered medical laboratory technician read this magazine regularly.
The magazine not only offers the basic knowledge in how to process human samples accurately but also publishes the latest news regarding new technologies. I have gained a lot of knowledge by reading this magazine. For example, I didn’t know how to identify white cells under a microscope. After reading the magazine in 2013, I found it was not a problem for me to identify different kinds of white cells during work. Also, the magazine helps me catch up with the new technology. 【P2】媒体类：喜爱的歌

Describe a favorite song of yours (a song that means something special to you)
  • What this song is about
  • When you listened to this song for the first time
  • How often you listen to this song
  • And explain why you think it is your favorite?
  1. 本题的A是a song，描述一首歌；B是favorite of yours, (that means something special to you)，限制情感倾向。
  2. 提纲建议：
    • 简述这首歌的歌名、演唱者、多久听一次等基本信息
    • 详述这首歌歌词的含义及其他你喜欢的部分
    • 总结和感受
  3. 时态建议：介绍歌曲基本信息用现在时，讲述自己第一次听的经历用过去时。
  4. 其他：这是描述歌曲的媒体类题目中最简单的一道，只限制了这首歌的情感倾向，即喜爱。括号中的低频版本相对难一点，要说出它对自己的特殊意义）。不要忘了，一首歌的元素非常多，不只有歌词，曲、编曲、演唱者，甚至MV或原声所在的电影电视剧都可以讲。

I’m gonna introduce one of the greatest hits in English music history of all times. It’s called Bohemian Rhapsody, by the one-and-only legendary British rock band Queen. You may have heard of it. It’s a six-minute suite, consisting of different sections without a chorus: an intro, a ballad segment, an operatic passage, a hard rock part and a reflective coda. It was written by Freddie Mercury, the lead singer somewhere in the 1970s and was reportedly the most expensive single ever made at the time of its release.

The first time I heard the song is in a TV show, American Idol, a singing competition. In this audition, Adam Lambert, the runner-up of that year sang this song with his dramatic voice and emotions. I was like, dumbed by it immediately. I heard something about a murderer confessing to his mom that he killed someone, or something. I searched the lyrics I caught, and it turned out to be the ballad segment of that song.

Having learned more about the song, I was amazed by how eclectic the composition is and how indescribably talented and fragile Freddie was. I couldn’t get enough of the ballad segment and I even tried an acoustic cover of it, with the guitar instead of the piano in original version.

I later learned that the murderer story was invented and somehow reflected the mental condition of Freddie back then and it’s said that the composer refused to explain his composition other than saying it was about relationships; the band is still protective of the song’s secret till today. In a random interview I knew that Freddie was a very complex person: flippant and funny on the surface, but he concealed insecurities and problems insquaring up his life with his childhood.

I was also, to some extent, traumatized in a way in my formative years and I guess that’s why I so relate to this song and even can’t dry my eyes listening to it at times.

inspirational／stimulant／high-quality lyrics／The song of the small crowd

The song ‘High Hopes’ sung by David Gilmour of Pink Floyd band is a song I have loved always and would hear often for the rest of my life. Pink Floyds is famous for their progressive and psychedelic music and high-quality lyrics. This song falls under the progressive rock music genre and was the 11th song on the album ‘Division Bell’. I usually listen to this song on my computer and sometimes in my MP3 player with the headphone. In fact, I am a great fan of this music band and most of their songs are on my favourite list. This song is specifically attractive for the music composition, lyrics and theme. This song reminds the past and compares how greener and full of possibility it was. The church bell and the lyrics make an out of the world illusion that only people who love it would realise. Every time I hear this song, I can feel my childhood, the sweet memory and the greener town I used to live. This is the song that opens the horizon for me to visit my past. I love this song from my heart. This music puts me in a sad yet charming mood. I feel sad that the past days won’t be coming back and I won’t have that childhood charm anymore. On the other hand, this song gives me a genuine pleasure of the memories of my childhood and all the friends and people I knew then.

【每日表达】句型、习语、小幽默

step up your game

bring you’re a-game

  • 对于很多游戏迷来说，人生可能也像游戏，要打怪升级，要想办法长经验，要充实自己的装备弹药库从而应对更多的挑战。在一些节骨眼上，我们的确需要升升级，这种升级，就叫step up your game. 而bring your a-game则指发挥出自己最好水平，把撒手锏都使出来。
  • In this competition, he really stepped up his game and won the champion. （他之前没有那么棒，这次“升级”了）
  • In this competition, he brought his a-game and won the champion. （他使出浑身解数，发挥到了最好）

all walks of life

jack-of-all-trades

  • 我们在表达中经常想说“各行各业的人”及“身兼数职的人”，这两个意思刚好可以用两个名词性的习语来表达，丝毫不差。All walks of life指的就是各个行业的人，是一群人；jack-of-all-trades则是指身兼数职的某个人，是一个人。注意有没有短线连接哦。
  • When the new sports center finished, all walks of life came to experience.
  • He can do stand-up comedy, ventriloquism, impression, and hosting. Such a jack-of-all-trades!

【P3技巧】推测类与对策类

第七类，预测类 Providing speculations

  • 将来时与虚拟语气
  • 开脑洞，先说现象再说原因
  • How do you see…changing in the future?
  • Do you think in the future people will…?
  • What do you think will happen to … in the future?
  • Is … going to be… in the future?
  • How will cellphones develop in the future?
  • Do you think TV will be replaced by computers?
  • What subjects will be more important than it is now in the future?
  • Do you think dress code will still exist in formal occasions?
  • What will happen to public transportation in the future?

第八类，对策类 Offering solutions

  • 未限制主体的，用主体分类；限制主体的，直接答
  • What can … do to…?
  • What can be done to…?
  • Are there any ways people can?
  • How can we…?
  • What should the government/citizens do to …
  • What measures/steps should … take to …
  • What measures should the government take to prevent kids from watching certain types of TV programs?
  • What can be done to attract more tourists to your hometown?
  • What would you do if you couldn’t make an important decision?
  • What can parents do to make children focus on their study better?
  • What can be done to protect endangered animals?
