Posted on 2018-08-11

【P1】爱好类：运动

运动是这个题季非常钟爱的话题，出现在好几个P1大题中。我将这些题目一并归于这一道中。命题角度从个人喜好到常见运动、受欢迎运动及运动员等，不一而足。

What kinds of exercise do you do?

Sports are my thing, and I do all sorts of them, ranging from individual and fundamental ones like jogging and swimming, to collective and competitive ones like basketball and soccer.

What was your favorite sport when you were young?

I used to be crazy about soccer in my younger days and I was the best striker my elementary school team has to offer.

Do you like extreme sports?

Nah, not really. Do I look like someone who’s into crazy stuff? When it comes to sports, it’s always better safe than sorry, isn’t it?

What kinds of exercise are popular in your country?

Some sports are universally popular, like basketball and soccer, and you do see young fans everywhere. It’s worth mentioning that table tennis, as the so-called national ball, is well-liked among all groups.

Do you think children should play sports regularly?

I surely do since children need to build up their physiques and exercise absolutely helps with that. Apart from that, team spirit and a sense of competition should also be cultivated.

What kinds of sports would you like to try in the future?

I really wanna try diving. It seems thrilling to jump from a 10-meter platform, turn some somersaults and plunge into water. Besides, divers look fit, man, with 6 packs and things. I want that too.

landlubber／sink like a rock

Do you have a favorite sports star?

I like a bunch of athletes like NBA stars Lebron James, Kobe Bryant and rising soccer star Kylian Mbappe. But I can’t say any of them is my favorite.

Do you like to watch sports on TV?

Not really. I don’t even own a tv set in my apartment. But I do watch some reruns of soccer or Olympic events on the Internet.

【P1】天气类：雨天

这次天气类考的是雨天。我给咱们国家下雨规律和趋势做了一个小结，利用季风的原理可以回答绝大部分题目。但各位练习的时候要结合自己家乡的实际情况，选取里面的话来答哦。每道单题都要练习。

Does it rain much in China? (Where and when?)

Is there any part of China where it doesn’t rain much?

When does it rain most in your hometown?

In which season does it rain most in other parts of China?

Where does it rain most in China?

The annual precipitation every year maintains a medium-level. But it rains much more in the South and East than in the North and West. The mainland China is largely affected by monsoons which in summer come from the Pacific and the Indian Ocean carrying water. That’s why the South and East enjoy constant rains in summer. But since monsoons are blocked by plateaus and mountains, it scarcely rains throughout a year in those landlocked provinces in the North and West. Monsoons come in reverse directions in winter, making it rain less across the country then.

Do you remember any time when it rained particularly heavily in your hometown?

It was like 15 years ago when 7 days of torrential rains caused flooding that killed dozens of people in village. Schools were closed and work suspended. Bittersweet.

【P2】物件类：最爱的季节

Describe your favorite season of a year

  • What it is
  • When it is
  • How the weather is like
  • Why you like this season
  • What activities you usually do during it
  • Why you like it
  • How different kinds of weather would affect people
  1. 本题的A是a season，描述一个季节；B是your favorite, 限制这个季节的属性。
  2. 提纲建议：
    • 简述该季节的时间和最大的特征
    • 详述该季节吸引人的地方
    • 总结感受，适当升华
  3. 时态建议：介绍季节本身的时间、特征用现在时，如果讲自己在该季节的经历，用过去时
  4. 其他：这是最经典的雅思P2之一，具有早期P2的特征——限制少，自由度高。选取某个季节的旅行计划、景物描述的素材，就可以匹配串题。但别忘了本题题目毕竟要求描述这个季节本身，因此第一段肯定要用一些语言叙述genera的东西。

Fall is my favorite season, by far, for aesthetic and personal reasons. it’s the third season in a year and it usually begins in September and ends in November. Well, at least here in Beijing. All the leaves from the trees and plants change into multi-colored works of art and fall away. It creates bare, and vulnerable branches, revealing the true scenery underneath. It also represents a beautiful cycle of loss, regeneration and regrowth once the spring season comes around. The dead leaves and branches on the ground disintegrate and turn into part of the soil, which are used as seeds and fertilizer later once the cold welcomes the warmer weather.

The fall is always full of magnificent colors. red and gold maples, brown and grey Chinese parasols, let alone colorful fruits and vegies, all interlocking pieces of the puzzle of nature. This season is perfect for taking beautiful scenic photos and spending time outdoors. Nothing can relax and rejuvenate more than enjoying nature and the surrounding scenery.

With cooler temperatures rolling in, saying goodbye to summer and hello to fall is not a hard thing to do. It’s a great time to break out the jackets, coats, boots, scarves and hats in preparation for the change in season. It’s also the perfect time to start making hot cocoa and lighting a fire in the fireplace. This season creates a sense of comfort, warmth and reflection. It’s a great time to go camping, fishing, go on a road trip or anything else where you can spend quality time with family and eat good food.

By the way, I was born in autumn equinox and I’m a Virgo. It’s said that people born around vernal and equinoxes are brighter and more sensible. I think it’s true, isn’t it?

【P2】物件类：童年玩具

Describe a toy you had (that you received) when you were a child.

  • What the toy was
  • When you had it
  • Who gave it to you
  • What it was like
  • How you used this
  • And explain how you felt about it.
  1. 本题的A是a toy，描述一个玩具；B是you had (that you received) when you were a child, 限制这个玩具的来历。
  2. 提纲建议：
    • 简述这个玩具的基本信息、特征、来历
    • 详述这个玩具的玩法和特别之处
    • 总结玩具和童年
  3. 时态建议：介绍玩具本身的信息和特征可以用现在时，如果玩具已经不在了也可以用过去时。讲述与玩具的互动，用过去时。述及对现在的影响，可以用现在完成时。
  4. 其他：这也是一个比较老的P2题目了，典型的状物类题目，一定要描述清楚这个玩具的基本信息和特征。另外，可以走温情路线，把童年啊亲人啊放进去。

I’d like to describe my only toy back in childhood. That’s right, the only plaything I’ve ever had. It was a jack in the box. From the outside, it’s one of the typical kinds. It outwardly consists of a box with a crank. When the crank is turned, a music box mechanism in the toy plays a melody. After the crank has been turned at some random times, the lid pops open and a clown pops out of the box. But the clown on the coiled spring is no regular Jack, it’s a clown that doesn’t have white-face make-up or wear pointed hats and ruffled collars, but only has a sorrowful look. To make it even more pathetic, it wasn’t from anyone. I found it at 10 in a clearing within one suburb of my hometown, dilapidated and dirty. Then I picked it up and brought it back home.

Wait, I forgot to tell you that it must have been produced here in China, cause the music it plays when you crank is nothing but the Chinese folk song, Jasmine. As I recall, I used to play with it after school, cranked out my Jack, greeted him with different questions like “How you doing today?” or “Are you feeling better?”, totally random questions. Then I would tell him what happened at school and things. Truth is I was an outsider back then and bullied sometimes, and I really enjoyed talking with the sad clown cause I knew that he wouldn’t judge, and he obviously was a great listener.

Not until my family moved to a new place recently did I realize that I had left him under my bed for so long I couldn’t remember how I had stopped talking to him. And I don’t know whether it’s that I had been a grown-up and viewed things differently or… I was taken aback when I cranked my Jack out after nearly 10 years. It was the creepiest thing I’d ever seen!

【P2】物件类：好照片

Describe a (good) photo you have taken (/of you taken by someone)

  • Where you took it
  • When you took the photo
  • What/who is in the photo
  • And explain how you felt about the photo
  • where it was taken
  • When it was taken
  • Who took it for you
  • And why it was unforgettable for you)
  1. 本题的A是a photo，描述一张照片；B是 (good), you have taken (/of you taken by someone), 限制这张照片的来历（和属性）

  2. 提纲建议：

    • 简述这张照片的来历、基本信息
    • 详述这张照片上的人和物、背后的故事
    • 总结自己的感受，适当升华

  3. 时态建议：描述照片上的画面用一般现在时，讲述照片拍摄时的状况及画面承载的故事用过去时。

  4. 其他：关于照片的P2题目非常多，这次出现了两个版本（括号内为低频版本），差别在于你是拍摄者还是照片中的主人公。为了应对这两个版本，可以更换照片主人公与拍摄者，也可以直接讲成一张自拍，搞定。

A good photo has always been a major throwback. You find yourself reminiscing about what happened back then and having vicarious happiness or sorrows. I’m not a photographer. Actually I even have never owned a camera. But I take some photos every now and then with my phone, some of which I feel proud of.

Like this one I took on an undisturbed narrow lane of a schoolboy in the shaggy blue uniform crouching and touching a puppy. And he is shedding tears. I captured this moment as I passed by, surprised by how agreeable this scene seemed. Then I completely forgot this snapshot until I flickered through my album the other day getting ready to release my storage.

And I bumped into this one once again before I zoomed it in and wondered why the kid was crying. I observed it quite meticulously and didn’t wanna miss anything. Then I thought, um, the puppy was a little weird. Something was missing. Then I zoomed in to its biggest, and I gulped.

The poor little thing has only three legs. Turned out the caring kid wasn’t crying over nothing. For the first time ever, I took it closer and had a thorough look of the picture. The puppy has an innocent face with a playful look, sadness rarely detected. He is staring at our schoolboy with his bulgy jade-green eyes. Standing beside the kid, he seems enjoying the fondling. But the kid’s face crumples, as if he lost his beloved toy.

I have no idea what became of the kid and the puppy, but the photo, I will keep it.

【每日表达】句型、习语、小幽默

a blessing in disguise

  • 我国古代有塞翁失马的故事，焉知非福。祸从天降，不一定就是祸，咬咬牙，坚持坚持，说不定福就在里面藏着呢。福兮祸所依，祸兮福所伏。这个习语讲的就是这件事，字面上看指伪装起来的祝福，指的就是乍看像是坏事或厄运，但实际上是好事和福气。
  • Losing my job was a blessing in disguise — I never would have found this one if it hadn’t happened.

Murphy’s law

  • 墨菲定律想必大家都比较熟悉了，大致指的是自己认为可能会出错的事，最终真的就会出错。如果把它当一个习语用在句子中做一个名词性成分，那么就是一种幽默的表达，很有意思。
  • Sounds like Murphy’s Law is working overtime! （妈的刚还在想会不会加班，你看吧！）

【综合技巧】5“W”1“H”拓展法

Would you like to ride a bike to work in the future?
I would like to ride my bike to work in the near future.
  • (what)
  • Since sharing economy is lit, many commuters turn to those shared bikes, scanning a QR code and here they go. Which I find convenient and eco-friendly. I wanna try that too.
  • (Why)
  • Because cycling for me will not only for transportation, but for fitness too. As so you know, I major in computer science, ant I bet my work after graduation will be so tiring I wouldn’t have time to work out. Besides, it’s eco-friendly, right?
  • (When)
  • …But only when the weather’s good. I mean, here in Hainan, probably means, never.
  • (Where)
  • …But only if my workplace is not far from home, like within 10 kilometers or so. Or otherwise I would be sweaty and stinky all day long.
  • (Who)
  • Since I will be sharing my apartment with another 2 or three roommates and I may not have an office of my own, riding to work can be the only time to be alone.
  • (How)
  • Since I’m terrific at cycling, riding to work will be my preference. I will be able to scuttle through the concrete jungle with ease.
