【P1】爱好类：户外活动

这个题季关于运动的题蛮多的，户外活动、身体训练、闲暇时光这些题都牵涉到运动，所以算P1的一个重点。除了经典了问喜好问时间之外，也需要思考一下运动给人带来的好处

What do you do in your spare time?

As a sports buff, I do various kinds of sports, be it collective ones like basketball or volleyball, and fundamental individual ones like jogging or swimming.

Do you like outdoor activities?

Outdoor activities are definitely my cup of tea, a major escape from my stationary work at office which never seems to end. Being outside alone is a breath of fresh air for most of modern people.

How often do you do that?

Pathetic as it may sound, my work takes up most of my time, even leisure time. But I’ve tried to squeeze in some time for exercises since I did the current job.

How much time do you spend outdoors every week?

If the two hours’ commute every day isn’t included, I would say maybe 30 minutes? I routinely take a stroll经常散步 with my wife after dinner.

What outdoor activities do you like to do (the most)?

Outdoor sports are my favorite. Hiking and boating always recharges my batteries on weekends. During weekdays, I often fantasize about scents气味 of flowers and chirping鸣叫 of birds.

【P2】地点类：需改进的场所

Describe a public place (facility) that you think needs improvements

  • Where the place is
  • What it is like
  • Why you think it needs improvements
  • When you go there
  • What you like and dislike in the public place
  • And explain how to make it better
  1. 本题的A是a place (facility)，描述地点；B是public, you think needs improvements，限制属性
  2. 提纲建议：
    • 描述此地点基本信息，位置、样貌、何时去，引出需改进的点
    • 详述改进的原因、方法
    • 评价改进的可能后果，适当升华
  3. 时态建议：描述地点特征时用一般现在时，讲改进的点也用现在时，讲之前的缺点用过去时，改进之后可用将来时，也可用虚拟语气。最后的评价如果涉及到类别问题，可用一般现在时；述及对现在的影响可用现在完成时。
  4. 其他：括号为不同版本。Facility = functional building. 从串题上讲，这道题与a building you dislike 基本可以匹配，只需要加上如何改进的表述即可。

I’d like to describe the artificial turf soccer pitch人造足球草坪 in my university. Since lately people around the world are crazy about the World Cup, this is the first thing that came into my mind. And without doubt, that dilapidated残破 facility really needs an overhaul翻修.

The soccer pitch is right in the center of the campus and not surprisingly has been the least visited place for a long time. I’m not a fan of soccer, but I pass by经过 this place a lot, and I’ve noticed the vicious恶毒 circle that the less it is used, the less possible that they will maintain it. Although this kind of makes me like this place as a great date option.

But not for soccer players of course, who have no other choice but play in the nearby municipal sports center and constantly complain about it. And it’s funny how disappointed you will be when you’re ready to play after class with your best buddies only to find the soccer pitch on campus is in a miserable condition.

It’s got this patchy turf, some parts even completely worn out, making the surface bumpy and when you run on it, you get this weird feeling that you may sprain your ankles and knees anytime. And the peripheral外围设别 guard net has tons of holes on it. So, watch out people passing by, cause you may get hit by a flying football.

Just think about it, the university decided to use synthetic合成的 grass instead of natural turf in the first place because it would be easier and much cheaper to maintain it, right? Then a complete fixup修理 won’t cost too much, all they need to do is to check and replenish补充 the turf in high use areas and fix the surrounding net. Doesn’t sound too much, does it? Or they can always just find a qualified groundsmen.

【P2】事件类：第一支手机

Describe the time you received your first cellphone (describe what difference it made when you first receive your phone)

  • When it was
  • Where you were
  • How you got it
  • Who gave it to you
  • What it was like
  • And explain how you felt about itc
  1. 本题的A是a time，描述事件；B是you received your first cellphone, 限制内容。实际上是一道事件+物品类题目。
  2. 提纲建议：
    • 获得第一支手机的时间、地点、方式、人物
    • 详述此手机的外观、功能（抓住特征，不要面面俱到）
    • 使用心得，可与现在的手机对比
  3. 时态建议：获得第一支手机的相关事件叙述用过去时，描述手机可用过去时也可用现在时。述及对现在的影响时用现在完成时。
  4. 其他：作为事件+物品类题目，要一手抓故事、一手抓物品，两手都要硬。若抽到括号中的版本，描述顺序一样，可以强调使用此手机带来的影响。

Oh my god! I love this topic! Just the other day, I smashed my iPhone and its screen was soon into pieces. And that reminded me of my first phone ever, a Nokia brick. That’s right, I call it a brick, cause it was so robust强大的 and reliable I threw it off mischievously欢畅 and it remained intact with only inconspicuous不显眼的 scratches刮痕. It was from my mom for my 15th birthday. I convinced her to get me a phone for a summer break trip to visit my cousin in college while I was still in high school. The phone, arguably, would keep me safe from all that evil partying. That black and white thing really made my high school colorful.

This Nokia was like THE coolest gadgets小工具 in school and many of my classmates lusted after喜欢 it. It was like the size of a clutch purse. But you could change the cover to like pink crystals or alien heads, or whatever. Features included calls, messages, stopwatch, alarm clock, remainders, you name it. But most of all, the ultimate planet saving game – Snake. And of course, I spent hours playing it which sounds stupid today, but I was extremely good at it cause they kept forgetting how an amazing piano player I was and therefore ambidextrous双手灵活的，怀有二心的.

It was not only me back then who called it a brick, but nearly all Chinese Nokia fans did. It was said that it made a perfect weapon, an expedient one in case of emergencies. Just take a look at these fragile models now, and you’ll understand why I miss it so much, not to mention it carried those golden memories of being a teenager.

【P2】事件类：改变计划

Describe a time you had to change your plan (you changed your mind)

  • When this happened

  • What made you change the plan

  • What the new plan was

  • And explain how you felt about the change

  1. 本题的A是a time，描述事件；B是you had to change your plan (you changed your mind)，限制事件内容。

  2. 提纲建议：

    • 用时间顺序，开门见山，先讲改变前
    • 改变中（改变的原因、过程）
    • 改变后（新计划、想法，感受，总结）
  3. 时态建议：若先引出改变本身，那改变前可用可去完成时，改变中用过去时，改变后可用过去时也可用一般现在时，述及对现在的影响可用现在完成时。若不引出改变本身，开门见山一来就讲故事的话，一律使用过去时。
  4. 其他：这是最纯粹的事件类题目，纯记叙文，一定按照时间顺序讲清楚，当然也可以倒叙，从改变后讲起，追溯改变前。讲故事要有细节，才能打动人心，也能把coherence的分得到。

Coincidentally, just last weekend my plans were ruined by the weather. The weekends are usually when I’ll travel back home and visit with my family, which was my plan for last weekend. I’d arranged my schedule and made sure I didn’t have any studying or something else pending. I’d work during the week to free up the 2 days, so I couldunwind and have some quality time with my parents.

But on Friday night, the weather started to change out of the blue and it looked ominous不详的. And by the evening, when I had planned to leave, there was this incredible storm developing. The rain was torrential滂沱 with a gusting wind and tons of roads were reported as being flooded and traffic stopped in various places in the city.

I immediately called home and spoke with my mom who told me it was the same there and that I shouldn’t travel in such miserable weather or I might get delayed. I was a bit sad, but the weather was so bad no one really wanted to go out at all. In fact, the journey from my university back to my apartment which was only like 10 minutes’ walk was horrendous可怕的 with terrible traffic, and I ended up completely soaked浸泡. I had to admit that it would not have been a good idea to travel all the way home.

Well, then I waited for a couple of hours to see if things would be better, but it didn’t, so I eventually gave up on the idea and started to think what I could do instead. Frustrated as I was, I managed to talk a few friends of mine to play a few rounds of a hit online game with me and I won three fourths of the rounds, which was a first. And of course I called my family again to check on them, my mom telling me she learned a new song and sang it over the phone, which was excellent.

【每日表达】句型、习语、小幽默

once in a blue moon

like getting blood from/out of a stone

  • 有些景观，难得一遇，遇上了千万不要错过。蓝月亮虽美，两至三年才能见到一次。不过，至少两三年还能有幸欣赏到。想从石头里得到血液，那基本就是不可能了。因此我们可以用这两个习语表示某件事发生的概率非常低，非常难得罕见。
  • I work in Beijing while my family all live in Lhasa, and we get to meet once in a blue moon.
  • He had never practiced before taking the test, and getting an 8 is like getting blood out of a stone.

last/final straw

  • 听过最后一根稻草压垮骆驼的故事没？最后一根稻草，就叫last或final straw, 你看简不简单，好不好用？你跟男朋友处得本来就不好，他爸妈也不喜欢你。最关键的，他还出轨了，这就是让你跟他分手的最后一根稻草。

  • He’d been unhappy at work for a long time but the last straw came when he was refused promotion.
