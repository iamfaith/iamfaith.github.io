IELTS written reminder

Posted on 2018-06-20

writing part one

vocabulary：reach/increase-rise/decrease-fall/grow/peak

  • increase dramatically/skyrocket up
  • rise steadily rise marginally(sightly)
  • decrease significantly
  • fall gradually

or

  • a dramatic increase
  • a steady rise
  • a significant decrease
  • a gradual fall

six categories(bar chart) every 1000 people = per 1000-population

analysis:

  • It is clear that… (overview)
  • Looking first at the older age groups,…
  • At the other end of the age scale,…
  • there was almost no difference
  • around/roughly/almost/approximately

strain/stress/pressure/majority/nature of problem(问题本质)／people mattered to them(对他们重要的人)
