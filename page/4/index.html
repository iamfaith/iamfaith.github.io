JavaScript深入之词法作用域和动态作用域

作用域

作用域是指程序源代码中定义变量的区域。

作用域规定了如何查找变量，也就是确定当前执行代码对变量的访问权限。

JavaScript 采用词法作用域(lexical scoping)，也就是静态作用域。

静态作用域与动态作用域

因为 JavaScript 采用的是词法作用域，函数的作用域在函数定义的时候就决定了。

Describe a visitor in your home (a time when someone visited your home)**

  • Who this visitor was
  • When he/she visited your home
  • What you did
  • Why he/she came to visit
  • And explain whether you like him/her or not
    提示：
  1. 本题的A是a visitor，描述人；B是in your home，限制地点。实质上是人物+事件结合类题目。
  2. 提纲建议：
    a) 描述这位visitor的基本信息，外貌、性格特征，以及事件时间地点
    b) 详述拜访过程、原因，把故事讲清
    c) 对ta的评价，适当升华
  3. 时态建议：描述人物特征时用一般现在时，讲故事用过去时，最后的评价如果涉及到类别问题，可用一般现在时；述及对现在的影响可用现在完成时。
  4. 其他：若抽到括号内题目，则可略过人物特征，直接讲故事。

You know how hard it is to deal with a bragger right? I’m gonna describe an unexpected visitor to my home last February who turned out an annoying one. It was Mrs. Huang, a family friend. She is a tall and gaunt woman in her fifties now and she always wears this tacky yellowish gem in front of her chest.

So, the awful story began on the last day of spring festival, or the Lantern’s Day, when traditionally customary visits are supposed to end. Our heroine dropped by out of the bluewhen my whole family were cleaning the living room. She barged through the door the second it was opened, rushed in and began bragging about her new property insurance. She tried to talk my mom into buying one. I was like, come on, are you a newly employed insurance saleswoman or what. Not a professional one, apparently. I couldn’t help grinning about this idea which she noticed and then turned to me. Uh-oh, here we go. It was no surprise that she began boasting about how brilliant her son was. So her son was going to get his green card and get married both in the summer, blah blah blah.
